# 🎯 ShootySquare

ShootySquare is a small 2D shooter made with Unity 🧩.
You play as a little cube 🟦 that can aim and shoot at other cubes 🟥 using your mouse 💥.
It’s a fast, minimalistic, and fun experience focused on precision and quick reflexes.

---

## 🕹️ Gameplay
- Move your cube around the arena  
- Aim with your mouse cursor  
- Shoot at enemy cubes to destroy them  

---

## 🧩 Features
- Simple 2D shooting mechanics  
- Clean and minimalistic visuals  
- Fast-paced cube combat  
- Made with Unity

## ⚙️ How to Play
1. Move the cursor to aim 🎯  
2. Click to shoot 💥  
3. Destroy as many cubes as you can!
